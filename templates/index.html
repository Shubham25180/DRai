What is the StudyNotes feature in DrAI?

The StudyNotes feature allows you to generate comprehensive notes on any medical topic instantly, with varying levels of detail to suit your study needs.

The mock test feature allows you to take customized quizzes on specific topics, helping you practice active recall, identify weak spots, and get used to the exam format.

Absolutely. DrAI is designed to be a 24/7 AI tutor, providing instant doubt clearance, notes, and mock tests, making it a perfect companion for self-study.
